Good to Know/Map

Life in Sweden is almost cashless. There are shops or restaurants that just accept card payment. Swish becomes more and more widespread. It is possible to pay in taxis, busses even in small bakeries by card.

In every railway station and airport, Eduroam is available. In most long-distance busses (e.g. Flygbussarna from the airport) and trains (e.g. Arlanda Express) there is free internet.

Sweden is part of the Schengen area.
